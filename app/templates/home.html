What is UbSRD?

At it's core UbSRD aims to provide a method of probing the various structural features of Ubiquitin and ubiqutin like molecules. The purpose of this website is to provide a frontend for simple browsing of the UbSRD database. To start, take a look at our overview page, which should give you a sense of what type of data is stored in the UbSRD database. For example analysis, look at our examples page.
