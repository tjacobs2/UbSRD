What is UbSRD?

At it's core UbSRD aims to provide a method of analyzing the various structural features of Ubiquitin and ubiqutin like molecules. The purpose of this website is to provide a frontend for simple browsing of the UbSRD database. To interact with the database online, look at the database browser page. To do more complex structural analysis, download the database and check out our example analysis page.

If you find the data contained in UbSRD useful, please reference our article.
